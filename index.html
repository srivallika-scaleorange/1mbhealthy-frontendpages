ABDM & NHA Integration

1MBHealthy is fully integrated with the Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM) and National Health Authority (NHA) infrastructure, enabling seamless storage, management, and sharing of health records through the Ayushman Bharat Health Account (ABHA). This empowers patients to securely share their medical history with doctors, streamlining healthcare delivery.

  • • Seamless ABHA Integration – Users can log into their ABHA account via 1MBHealthy to securely store and access digital health records, eliminating the need for physical documents.
  • • Interoperable Healthcare Ecosystem – Leverages ABDM’s framework to connect with hospitals, labs, pharmacies, and doctors for efficient data sharing and care coordination.
  • • Empowered Patients and Doctors – Patients can share previous health data with doctors instantly, enabling faster diagnoses and personalized treatment plans.
  • • Compliance and Security – Built on ABDM’s HIPAA-compliant architecture, ensuring data privacy and adherence to NHA’s regulatory standards.
